# 🎵 Moodix - AI-Powered Mood-Based Music Player  

Moodix is an intelligent music player that **curates playlists based on your mood!** Using AI-driven mood detection, it analyzes **facial expressions, text input, or voice tone** to suggest the perfect tracks for your emotions.  

---

## 🚀 Features  
✅ **AI Mood Detection** – Detects emotions from images, text, or voice input.  
✅ **Smart Playlist Generation** – Auto-suggests songs for moods like Happy, Sad, Relaxed, Energetic, etc.  
✅ **Seamless Integration** – Works with local files, Spotify, and YouTube.  
✅ **Minimalist UI** – Simple and user-friendly design for smooth navigation.  
✅ **Customizable Preferences** – Manually adjust moods and refine recommendations.
